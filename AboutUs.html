Who We Are

Happy Treats is a local family owned small business with a vision of providing healthy and eco friendly dog chew to your fur babies. Our products are originated in the Himalayan mountains; made of yak and cow milk, which make it easy to digest. Our chews are 100% natural, long lasting and it is an excellent nutritional supplement for any size adult dog. Happy Treats is rich in calcium, proteins and low in lactose., which contributes to healthy teeth and gum.
