"Our nutritional Happy Treats snack is not intended to replace a full meal for your dog. It is a complementary food item that is designed to be given in conjunction with your dog's regular diet. To get the most enjoyment out of our Happy Treats bar, we suggest allowing your dog to chew on it for a while, depending on their appetite and chewing style, and then taking it away until the next time. This way, the Happy Treats bar can last for several days or even weeks, making it a long-lasting and enjoyable snack for your furry friend. 

It's important to supervise your dog while they are chewing on the Happy Treats bar, just like any other chewable item. By doing so, you can ensure that they are enjoying it safely and not accidentally swallowing large pieces.
